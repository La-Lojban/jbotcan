What is an example of "satci"s x2? Thank you.

>> No.980  

le stuzi cu satci ci mitre
"The location is exact to within three meters."

>> No.981  

>>980

"lo ci mitre" or better "lo mitre be li ci".

"ci mitre" says that there are exactly three meters (three meter-long things) such that the location is exact to within each one of them.

I suppose, strictly speaking, it should be "le stuzi cu satci lo mitre be li su'eci". Would "loi su'eci mitre" also be accurate? I'm a big fan of being able to write things concisely, as long as the semantics remain accurate.
